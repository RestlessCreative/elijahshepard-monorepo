For this project, I created a robust suite of custom components to provide Kelly with most flexibility possible. Each component has multiple levels of customization and can be added to any page. As a result, the site is almost 95% dynamic, controlled via a headless CMS. The front end is static, rendered via HUGO.

In case you missed it, ABC just aired the first season of Kelly Slater's "The Ultimate Surfer" (https://abc.com/shows/the-ultimate-surfer) which takes place at Surf Ranch.

Visit http://kswaveco.com to see this site I built in the wild.
