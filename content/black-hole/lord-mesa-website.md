Lord Mesa is one talented dude and an all-around good guy. As an illustrator, he has a massive following. Lord is famous for his chibi style drawings and humorous narratives on pop culture, movies, and tv. He asked me to create a web presence to serve as a hub for his fans. For the first time, he has a robust and dynamic website which chronicles all of his work and allow fans to buy his swag and products as well. The site is full of animation to make it fun and I built a bunch of custom admin tools for him to manage the content easily. Case in point: since launch, he has added several hundred new pages.
