I created sound design for some of the UI screens in this game as well as storyboarded some of the credit screens and co-directed the stage shoot for the live-action inserts we used in the credit screens. I did he BizDev on this one too, because of my relationship with EA Games. I coordinated and managed the video shoot which was fun because I got to work with **Maria Menounos** and **Natasha Bedingfield** who were the *"bond girls"* on this one and a belly dancer - *of course*.
