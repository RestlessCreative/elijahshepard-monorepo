I was asked to reinvent James' Web presence, to create a more engaging and robust destination for information. The site is a beast, consisting of more than 400 pages, book pages available in multiple languages, a responsive user experience, media, and exclusive hidden content for his vast community of fans. A massive suite of custom admin tools was developed to control almost every aspect of the site, architected with Wordpress and Pods. In addition, as part of the effort, a re-branding was also undertaken; resulting in a new logo and by extension a refresh of other online/offline collateral/communication elements. James Rollins is the author of the bestselling Sigma Force series (Sandstorm, Map of Bones, Black Order, The Judas Strain, The Last Oracle, The Doomsday Key, The Devil Colony, Bloodline and The Eye of God); six individual adventure thrillers; the blockbuster movie novelization, Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull; Tucker Wayne Series, The Sanguines Series, Jake Ransom series, The 6th Extinction, and others.
