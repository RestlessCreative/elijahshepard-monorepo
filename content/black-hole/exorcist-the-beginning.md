This was a fun one. I also submitted music to be considered as the film's composer but alas... At least I was able to set the mood for the site, including music and sound design. This site was more of an experience than informational, as you can probably tell. I don't have archived video of this site unfortunately. Shane took some great screenshots though. Shane and Mark kicked ass on this site once again. As an anecdote, I setup a stage shoot and borrowed a friend's daughter for a lazy-susan style video capture of her pretending to pray - which we composited into one of the scenes in the site.
